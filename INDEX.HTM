About Life Care Hospitals

Life Care Hospitals was established in 1995 with a vision to provide world-class healthcare services to all sections of society. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted healthcare providers in the region.

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by highly qualified healthcare professionals dedicated to patient care. We believe in treating patients with compassion and respect while delivering the highest standard of medical care.

With over 500 beds, 50+ specialties and 1000+ healthcare professionals, Life Care Hospitals is committed to medical excellence, innovation and patient satisfaction.
